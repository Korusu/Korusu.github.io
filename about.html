Chris Swingler - Back Story

 

Childhood in Zurich & Istanbul.

University in Sheffield. Highlights:
    * DJ for the Friday night Discos in Europe's largest hall of residence
    * Worked on 2 productions in the Theatre Group with Eddie Izzard!

Early career as a Programmer in London and Reading with CAP Group plc.

First career move to work in Pre-Sales for Boston MA based Prime Computer, first in the London office and then in the Paris office.

In 1991, I was offered a role as a Consultant and Trainer for Progress Software Corp. (NASDAQ PRGS) in the Paris office. I worked for clients all over France and twice for the water company in Abidjan, Ivory Coast.

In 1996, I left Progress to start my own consulting and services business working for Specsavers Retail Systems for 4 years.

Subsequently, I worked for many clients in France, UK, Italy, Germany, Turkey & USA including:
* NCR EMEA, WITS, Netsetup, RJW Services, Domilog, Kantena, CNAF 
* IAP, Atuana, Ulukom, Aptiva  
* The White Company, HP EMEA, Rhodes Events, Winterbird Interiors 

This first phase of my career was very technical in roles including Programmer, Pre-sales, Trainer, Consultant and Project management. During this time I also worked for the organic vineyards of Correns in the heart of green Provence, the first organic village in France and opened the market in the UK for their fine Decanter award winning wines.

After a very techincal career, I moved to work in international sales for IAP GmbH in Hamburg and secured new clients for them in France, Italy & Turkey. I then moved to open up a new business to help companies with Italy Market Entry for which I researched, recruited and retained three business partners in Bologna whom I am still in contact with if anyone needs this service.

In recent years, I have worked on many creative projects. These have now become brands that you can see on this website.

I have three children, Eleanor who is 8, Melvyn 24 and Richard 25. My father is doing very well at 91 :-) My mother very sadly passed away 20 years ago.

In the 1950's Chris's Mum and Dad drove from London to Antibes via Geneva on their Vespa called "Canard". Mum called it Canard, the French word for Duck, because she thought it looked like a Duck!

Photo taken in Antibes, France around the mid 1950's.

John Swingler (Chris's Dad) and Pervenche Verdier (Chris's Mum) got married in 1956. They were together for 44 years before mum sadly passed away in July 2000.
