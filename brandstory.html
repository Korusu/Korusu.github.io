Korusu - Brand Story

Korusu is the Turkish word for Grove

I got the idea for the name over 2 days of brain-storming and remembering where I used to live as a child in Istanbul.
We lived on a steep street overlooking the Bosphorous near the Rumeli Hisar castle. It was called Ayse Sultan Korusu and the thought of naming the home of my creative brands with a name that came directly from where my home was in those years and evoking thoughts of Fruit Trees in a Grove and using the Turkish word became very appealing.

I think it sounds good too, like "Chorus" with (a) "u" added :-)
