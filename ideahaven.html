My first project was to write and narrate stories for my daughter Eleanor.

Baltimore and the pop-up radio station
An album of 6 short stories all about a Teddy Bear called Baltimore who becomes a Radio DJ and broadcasts from a radio station which pops up out of his suitcase! What fun! I have also created activities to accompany the stories. ‍

Find out more on www.ideahaven.uk
where you will find clips, activities to download and how to buy the written and/or audio versions of the stories.

My second project is currently in production and due out before end of June 2020.

‍Focus in the Wild
‍An education resource for Children (ages 7 - 11) learning at home
‍‍
My friend Philippe and I thought that we could produce a resource for children stuck at home who would like to learn more about World Wildlife from someone who has been to many hidden places around the world and taken some amazing pictures. Focus in the Wild was born out of this idea and will soon be available for everyone for free on request.

Philippe Ricordel, based in Paris, is a World Wildlife photographer and has taken many people on photo safaris to see wildlife in remote corners of the world including Japan, New Zealand, Norway, Brazil, Rwanda and Kenya.
