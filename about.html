Chris Swingler - Back Story

 

Childhood in Zurich & Istanbul.

University in Sheffield. Highlights:
    * DJ for the Friday night Discos in Europe's largest hall of residence
    * Worked on 2 productions in the Theatre Group with Eddie Izzard!

Early career as a Programmer in London and Reading with CAP Group plc.

First career move to work in Pre-Sales for Boston MA based Prime Computer, first in the London office and then in the Paris office.

I was then offered a role as a Consultant and Trainer for Progress Software Corp. (NASDAQ PRGS) in the Paris office. I worked for clients all over France and twice for the water company in Abidjan, Ivory Coast.

After nearly 6 years with Progress France, I left to start my own consulting and services business working initially for Specsavers Retail Systems for 4 years. Subsequently, I worked for many clients in France, UK, Italy, Germany, Turkey & USA including: 
* NCR EMEA, WITS, Netsetup, RJW Services, Domilog, Kantena, CNAF 
* IAP, Atuana, Ulukom, Aptiva  
* The White Company, HP EMEA, Rhodes Events, Winterbird Interiors 

This first phase of my career was very technical in roles including Programmer, Pre-sales, Trainer, Consultant and Project management. During this time I also worked for the organic vineyards of Correns in the heart of green Provence, the first organic village in France and opened the market in the UK for their fine Decanter award winning wines.

After a very techincal career, I moved to work in international sales for IAP GmbH in Hamburg and secured new clients for them in France, Italy & Turkey. I then moved to open up a new business to help companies with Italy Market Entry for which I researched, recruited and retained business partners in Bologna.

I have a daughter and 2 adult sons who are both graduates.

Chris's Mum and Dad drove from London to Antibes via Geneva on their Vespa called "Canard". Mum called it Canard, the French word for Duck, because she thought it looked like a Duck!
